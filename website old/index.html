8-Agent AI Swarm

Not a Bot. A Swarm.

Eight specialized agents run in parallel on every invoice. Each agent contributes its verdict. No single point of failure. No black box.

01
DocumentIntelligenceAgent
OCR extraction with vendor-adaptive learning. Gets faster and more accurate with every invoice from each supplier.
02
FraudDetectionAgent
7+ fraud checks per invoice. BEC patterns, velocity anomalies, duplicate detection, new payee flags. Risk score 0–1.
03
ValidationAgent
Enforces business rules: mandatory fields, date logic, currency consistency, Tax ID format, payment term validity.
04
MatchingAgent
2-way (PO) and 3-way (PO + GRN) matching with per-category tolerances. Line-by-line variance reporting.
05
ApprovalRoutingAgent
Determines the correct approver by amount, type, department, and entity. Enforces SLAs and auto-escalates.
06
ExceptionResolutionAgent
Auto-resolves common exceptions — minor PO variances, missing PO suggestions — without human intervention.
07
ERPPostingAgent
Formats and posts approved invoices directly to Oracle, SAP, NetSuite, Dynamics 365, and JDE. Returns transaction IDs.
08
RAGAssistantAgent (Aria)
Answers any AP question in plain English using your company's policy documents + platform knowledge. Voice-enabled.
